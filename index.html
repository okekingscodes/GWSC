Our Physical Facilities

Highly Equipped Hostels

Discover a perfect blend of adventure and comfort at our well-equipped swimming camping hostels. Cozy accommodations, modern facilities, high-speed Wi-Fi, and stunning nature views ensure a memorable stay. Friendly staff are always available to assist, making your experience truly unforgettable.

Dive into relaxation and healing at our medicated swimming pool. With specialized treatments, the pool offers therapeutic benefits for body and mind. Experience the soothing effects of our unique facility, designed to enhance your well-being and leave you refreshed after each swim.
